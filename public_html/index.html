HalLeiLujah is a weekend and part of the IAESTE summer programme exclusively for IAESTE students in Germany.
It is organized by IAESTE LC Halle and IAESTE Leipzig.

This weekend focuses on a mixture of culture and sports. You will be presented with an extensive programme. We care for your food, accommodation and transport in Halle and Leipzig, you enjoy the cities.

Friday, July 15 17:00 - Sunday, July 17 14:00

HalLeiLujah takes place starting from Friday, July 15, 2016 17:00 on and ends on Sunday, July 17, 2016 14:00. We recommend arriving on Friday, July 15 at about 17:00 but it won't be a big deal if you are later.

programme

Arrival in Leipzig

At first, you would need your eyes and spirit to get used to staggering beauty of Leipzig; that’s why we accommodate you in a hostel. After you have calmed your body, adapted but still shaking out of joy, we will visit the highest point in Leipzig (and possibly the world), MDR tower. A continuous looking on Leipzig from bird’s eye view for too long can damage your eyes (your brain just cannot conceive such a magnificent picture so you cannot even blink). So the rest of the evening we will spend together eating dinner, playing, conducting conversations about the magnificent view upon MDR tower.

Saturday morning

The day after, we will have breakfast and get ready to gaze upon further beauties of Leipzig. The best way to encompass as much as we can and not fall into a stupor is to have City Rally (exposal to uncontrollable amount of awesomeness of Leipzig causes stupor, scientifically documented). During the City Rally in the centre of Leipzig we will discover famous St. Nicholas Church, St. Thomas Church, Gewandhaus with University campus, the Augustusplatz with Opera. Afterwards, we will visit the Monument to the Battle of the Nations, one of the greatest monuments in Europe.

Photo "Monument to the Battle of the Nations": André Karwath [CC BY-SA 2.5]

Saturday afternoon

Finally, we will say goodbye to Leipzig, but grieve not, my friend, because we will continue our journey and admiration of the beautiful in Halle! Everyone will have a lunch pack to fortify oneself before canoe tour in Halle's green heart so that you can have different perspective on beauty of Halle, namely from water. We will row through Halle Giebichenstein, Halle’s own “little Michigan”. By the way, the admiration requires a lot of energy so we will eat in the green. We will be grilling and we will be playing in the park at the river Saale. And then, we will delve into mysterious Halle under the cover of the night while receiving stories and anecdotes, stretching over generations.

Sunday

The day after, we will have breakfast of course. At this point of journey, we think we do not have to mention how amazing Halle is and how much energy and emotions it consumes to delight in merely being in there. The first stop this day will be Halloren Chocolate Factory, the oldest one in Germany and also the best in the galaxy. The second, optional, stop will be Händelhaus, once a home of Barock genius. The last, optional stop will be the ruins Giebichenstein Castle, which ancient walls witnessed history of Halle for 11 centuries.

Fin

Unfortunately, there our journey will come to an end, but hold your tears and just try to grasp the splendour of Halle. With nostalgic smile and warmth in our souls, we will tell our children how once we visited Leipzig and Halle and it was amazing and it inspired us come back numerous times and maybe live in Leipzig or Halle forever and establish your own clan here.
